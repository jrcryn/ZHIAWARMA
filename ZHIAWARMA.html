    Our Story

    Marites Bugarin is a full-time mom and an entrepreneur based in Calamba City, Laguna. Being a mom and, as time went on, grasping the knowledge and ability to deliciously cook, Marites took advantage of her abilities. In 2023, she started her shawarma business called ZHIAWARMA, named after her loving daughter zhia, now offering delicious shawarma delicacies that are homemade and crafted with years of experience in cooking.
